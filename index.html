Lip Blush

Lip blushing is a relatively painless procedure that can be completed in about 2 hours. There is some swelling and redness immediately after the procedure, but this typically goes away within a few days. You will need to avoid wearing lipstick for about a week after your treatment. The results of lip blushing can last for up to 2 years. Over time, the pigment will fade slightly, but you can always get a touch-up treatment to maintain your desired look.

Eyebrows

Microblading is a semi-permanent makeup procedure that uses a small tool to create hair-like strokes in the eyebrow area. The result is a natural-looking, fuller brow that can last for up to 2 years. Microblading is a great option for people who want to enhance their natural brow shape, fill in sparse or overplucked brows or create a fuller, more defined brow

Benefits of SPMU

Waterproof

Semi-permanent makeup is waterproof and smudge-proof, so you can wear it through sweat, tears, and rain. This is a great option for people who are active, who live in a humid climate or who do a lot of watersports.

Saves Money

Semi-permanent makeup can save you time and money in the long run, as you won't have to spend time applying makeup every day. You'll also save money on makeup products, as you won't need to buy them as often.

Long Lasting

Semi-permanent makeup can last for up to 2-3 years, so you won't have to worry about reapplying it as often as traditional makeup. This can be a great option for people who are busy or who don't like to wear makeup every day.

Natural

Semi-permanent makeup is designed to look natural, so you won't have to worry about it looking fake. The pigments used for SPMU are also designed to fade over time, so you can get touch-ups as needed.
