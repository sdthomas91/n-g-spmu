Services

 

Lip Blush

Lip blushing is a relatively painless procedure that can be completed in about 2 hours. There is some swelling and redness immediately after the procedure, but this typically goes away within a few days. You will need to avoid wearing lipstick for about a week after your treatment. The results of lip blushing can last for up to 2 years. Over time, the pigment will fade slightly, but you can always get a touch-up treatment to maintain your desired look.

 

Eye Liner

If you are looking for a long-lasting, natural-looking way to enhance the appearance of your eyes, semi-permanent eyeliner is a great option. Contact us today to learn more about our semi-permanent eyeliner services and to schedule a consultation. If you are interested in learning more about semi-permanent eyeliner, please contact us today to schedule a consultation.

 

Eyebrows

Microblading is a semi-permanent makeup procedure that uses a small tool to create hair-like strokes in the eyebrow area. The result is a natural-looking, fuller brow that can last for up to 2 years. Microblading is a great option for people who want to enhance their natural brow shape, fill in sparse or overplucked brows or create a fuller, more defined brow
